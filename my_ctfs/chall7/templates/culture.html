Culture of the Motherland

🎭 Arts & Music

Russia is known for its world-class ballet, symphonic orchestras, and legendary composers like Tchaikovsky and Rachmaninoff.

🍲 Cuisine

From borscht to blini, the flavors of Russia are deep, soulful, and hearty—just like her people.

🐻 Symbols

The bear is not just a symbol of strength. It’s a spirit of resilience, warmth, and national pride.

🎉 Traditions

Celebrations like Maslenitsa and New Year’s Eve bring families and entire towns together for joy, food, and ice-cold winters warmed by fire and song.
