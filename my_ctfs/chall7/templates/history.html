History of Mother Russia

Our Glorious Past

From the ancient Rus' to the mighty Soviet era, and into the modern Federation — Russia has stood as a symbol of resilience, power, and soul. Her lands have seen empires rise, battles fought for freedom, and cultures flourish like the great birch forests of the north.

In 1917, the people’s revolution shook the world. In 1945, we stood tall in victory against darkness. And even now, our legacy continues to inspire generations.
